⚠ Important Exchange Rules

  • Disputes: All management decisions are final. We retain the right to void any bet matching in case of obvious errors or technical failures.
  • Settlement: Matches will be settled according to official governing body results (e.g., ICC). Any overturned decisions post-settlement will not affect payouts.
  • Odds Integrity: In cases of suspected market manipulation or syndication, accounts will be suspended, and open positions voided.
  • Market Closure: Under severe weather delays or match abandonment, matching will follow standard DLS par score guidelines or be refunded.
  • Agent Liability: Clients are responsible for ensuring sufficient available balance before entering positions. Agents cannot reverse confirmed executed orders.
